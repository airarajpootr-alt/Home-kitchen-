Chicken Biryani

A traditional South Asian rice dish packed with spices and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala & whole spices

Method

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  3. Cook rice until 70% done.
  4. Prepare chicken gravy.
  5. Layer rice and chicken, then steam.
